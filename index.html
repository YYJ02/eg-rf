About

EverGreat is a manufacturer and designer of high quality Antennas, RF Connectors and Cable Assemblies for use with industrial, communication and computer devices. Our main product line is Antennas such as Communication Antennas, Wireless LAN Antennas, Mobile cellular Antennas, GPS Antennas, and more. We also manufacture a wide range of machined RF connectors. With our knowledge and long experience with custom designed RF Antennas, we can assist you in your product development.

EverGreat can also help you with sourcing of Antenna Components since we are strategically located in Taiwan close to the most cost effective component manufacturers in Asia. All Evergreat is products are designed to enable easy installation of factory fitted or aftermarket accessories. We want the consumers to enjoy the luxury of life through safe & good quality communication. EverGreat is a ISO 9001 certified company. If you have any question give us a call and we'll be happy to tell you more.

弘益科技有限公司創立於2006年，本公司研發在天線設計領域專研多年，並已協助客戶開發多種天線產品...舉凡手機內建式天線，外掛式天線，TVB-T車用主動式數位天線。

為因應多元化的市場需求，本公司也協助客戶開發相關週邊產品ODM、OEM的部分，包含客戶所需求的RF connector、coaxial cable、cable assembly及一些特殊規格的料件部品。
